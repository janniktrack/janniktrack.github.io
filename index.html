I'm a Sociology PhD Student at the European University Institute (EUI) in Florence, Italy. This fall I'm visiting the Princeton Sociology Department.

My main research interests are in parental investment and child development. In my dissertation project I study how resources are distributed within the family and whether parental investment in children differs across siblings in the same family.
